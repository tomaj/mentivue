# Mentivue - Phased Go-to-Market Strategy

**Companion to SUBSCRIPTION.md, PRD.md** - rieši kritickú obchodnú otázku:

> **Ako začnem realisticky, ale postavím Mentivue na subscription-first model bez vyhorenia?**

Tento dokument definuje **4-fázovú stratégiu** ktorá:
- Začína extrémne lacno (Tomas čas + €100/mes infra)
- Validuje product-market fit pred capital investment
- Postupne pridáva subscription elements ako rastie traction
- Drží Tomas-time pod 15h/týždeň prvé 6 mesiacov
- Achievuje VC-grade subscription business do Y2

---

## 1. Strategic principle - "Earn the right to subscribe"

Klienti **neprídu na subscription model** ak im najprv nedokážeš že vieš dodať jednorázovú hodnotu. Subscription = trust + predictable value. Bez prvého nie je druhé.

```
[Phase 1]  Free Content + One-off Audits  →  Build authority
[Phase 2]  + Lightweight Subscription      →  Test recurring willingness  
[Phase 3]  + Full Pro Subscription         →  Scale recurring revenue
[Phase 4]  + Enterprise + Custom           →  Premium expansion
```

Každá fáza **stojí na ramenách predchádzajúcej**. Neskoč rovno do Phase 3.

## 4. PHASE 2: Lightweight Subscription (Mesiace 3-6)

**Hypotéza ktorú validujeme:** Klienti zaplatia €490/mes za continuous AI search intelligence.

### 4.1 Produktové portfolio expansion

**NEW IN PHASE 2:**
- ✓ **Mentivue Watch (€490/mes)** - lightweight subscription
- ✓ Customer onboarding flow (form fill, kickoff email)
- ✓ Branded monthly Health Snapshot PDF (5 strán, auto-generated)
- ✓ Email alerts pre signifikantné SoV shifts

**STILL NOT YET:**
- ✗ Strategy calls (príliš time-intensive)
- ✗ Slack integration
- ✗ Action reports (15 strán/mes too heavy)
- ✗ Pro a Enterprise tiers

### 4.2 Prečo Watch first?

Watch je **najlacnejší pre teba aj klienta**:

**Pre teba:**
- Mesačný PDF = automated z templates (1-2h klient setup, potom zero-touch)
- Alerts = automatizované cez cron + email
- No strategy calls = no Tomas-time scale problem
- Klient self-service, low support overhead

**Pre klienta:**
- €490/mes je experimentation budget (CMO si to dovolí bez CFO approval)
- Low commitment = low cancel friction
- "Try it, see if it's useful"

### 4.3 Watch packaging - presne čo dostanú

```
WEEKLY (každý štvrtok):
- Mentivue Pulse newsletter - rovnaký ako free version
  ALE s "Your Brand" personalizovanou sekciou (1 odsek)

MONTHLY (1. v mesiaci):
- 5-page Health Snapshot PDF
  Page 1: Cover s ich brand + Mentivue Index Score
  Page 2: SoV trend (30 days)
  Page 3: Top 3 movements (positive + negative)
  Page 4: Top 3 competitor moves
  Page 5: Single priority recommendation

REAL-TIME:
- Email alert ak SoV shift > 5pp WoW
- Email alert ak negative sentiment surge
- Max 2-3 alerts/mes (no spam)
```

**Kľúčový dizajn:** Watch je **80% automated + 20% Tomas review**. Maximum 30 min Tomas time per klient per month.

### 4.4 Tomas weekly v Phase 2

```
PONDELOK (3h):
- Same ako Phase 1, plus:
- Review Watch klientov Health Snapshots (final QA)

UTOROK-ŠTVRTOK (večery, 3h/deň = 9h):
- Pending audits (still primary revenue)
- 1× quick check-in email s Watch subscriber

PIATOK (3h):
- Watch klient onboarding ak je nový
- Monthly Action Snapshots final review (last week of month: 3h batch)
```

**Total: 14-15h/týždeň.** Stále zvládnuteľné.

### 4.5 Subscription operational requirements

**Tech needs:**
- Stripe Billing setup (subscription management, automated renewals)
- Klient identifikácia v DB (brand ID, subscription tier)
- Automated PDF generation per klient (monthly cron)
- Email sequencer (Resend) pre onboarding flow

**Time investment:**
- Setup: 20-30h jednorázovo (Week 6-7 build)
- Per-klient onboarding: 1h
- Per-klient monthly: 30 min review
- Per-alert: 5 min validate

## 5. PHASE 3: Full Pro Subscription (Mesiace 6-12)

**Hypotéza ktorú validujeme:** Klienti zaplatia €1 490/mes za actionable monthly intelligence + advisory.

### 5.1 Produktové portfolio expansion

**NEW IN PHASE 3:**
- ✓ **Mentivue Pro (€1 490/mes)** - flagship subscription
- ✓ Mesačný Action Report (15 strán, custom per klient)
- ✓ Quarterly Full Audit (35 strán)
- ✓ Quarterly 60-min strategy call
- ✓ Slack/email priority support (24h response)
- ✓ Custom monitoring prompts (klient špecifické)
- ✓ AI vs Paid Search math monthly refresh

### 5.2 Pro packaging - critical decisions

**Klučové operational decisions:**

**Action Report:** semi-automated
- 70% AI-generated from templates (Claude Sonnet)
- 25% client-specific data (auto-pulled from DB)
- 5% Tomas final review + insight customization
- Total time: 3-4h per klient per month

**Strategy Call:** quarterly
- Calendar booking via Calendly
- Tomas time: 60-min call + 30-min prep + 15-min follow-up = 1.75h
- Per quarter per klient

**Slack support:**
- Shared Slack channel ak klient má (preferred)
- Inak email
- 24h SLA on responses
- Max 4-5 questions/mes per klient (defined)

### 5.3 Tomas weekly v Phase 3

```
PONDELOK (4h):
- Review weekend dashboard
- 2-3 outreach emails (audit + Pro tier sales)
- Pulse newsletter prep

UTOROK-ŠTVRTOK (večery, 3-4h/deň = 10-12h):
- Pending audits
- Action Reports v rotation (5-6 klientov/mes = 1 týždenne)
- Slack response time

PIATOK (4h):
- QA + send Action Reports
- Strategy calls (rotujúce, ~1 týždenne)
- Watch klient monthly snapshots

WEEKEND (občas 2-3h):
- Pulse newsletter content
- Strategic thinking
```

**Total: 22-25h/týždeň** = už je to **vážny commitment**. Tu si v rozhodovacom bode:
- **Hire jr. analyst?** (€800-1500/mes part-time)
- **Quit Telekom?** (full-time commitment)
- **Cap klientov?** (5-10 Pro klientov max, žiadny ďalší rast)

### 5.4 Critical hire decision

**Pri 15-20 Pro klientoch potrebuješ pomoc.** Možnosti:

**Option A: Junior analyst (€600-1200/mes part-time)**
- Robí 1st-pass Action Reports drafts
- Tomas finalizes & adds insight
- Releases Tomas time: ~50%

**Option B: Outsource to AI agency**
- Hire content writer pre Pulse
- Cost: €500-1000/mes
- Risk: kvalita

**Option C: Build more automation**
- 2 týždne dev time
- Cost: €0 ale lost klient time
- Releases Tomas time: ~30%

**Recommendation:** Najprv investovať do Option C (automation), potom Option A (jr. analyst) keď growth oprávňuje.

## 8. Why this beats alternatives

### 8.1 vs "Subscription-first from Day 1"

**Subscription-first risks:**
- Tomas burns out (150h/mes content při Y1 target)
- Premature scaling without validated product
- Klient expects sophisticated product, gets MVP
- High CAC bez established authority
- Likely fails by month 6

**Phased hybrid:**
- Validates demand before commitment
- Builds authority before sells
- Scales operations gradually
- Risk-mitigated cash flow

### 8.2 vs "One-off-only forever"

**One-off-only ceiling:**
- ~€500k/year max (you can't deliver more than X audits/year alone)
- No recurring revenue = no enterprise value
- Linear scaling = burnout
- No exit value beyond consulting practice
- Easy for competitor to disrupt

**Phased hybrid:**
- Y3 €8-12M ARR = €40-80M valuation
- Recurring revenue = compounding growth
- Subscription stickiness = competitor moat
- Exit candidate for strategic acquirer

### 8.3 vs "Build full subscription product, then launch"

**Build-first risks:**
- 6+ mesiace dev time, zero revenue
- Build features klient doesn't want
- Cash flow death
- Out of energy at launch time

**Phased hybrid:**
- Revenue from week 6
- Features driven by real klient demand
- Cash flow positive from month 1
- Energy preserved through gradual build

## 10. Reality checks - kedy spomalíť alebo zastaviť

### 10.1 Phase 1 red flags (zastav, neprejdi)

- < 100 Pulse subscribers po 2 mesiacoch (no demand)
- 0 audit sales after 6 weeks (no willingness to pay)
- Negative feedback on free Industry Report (low quality)
- Tomas already burning out (sustainability)

**Action:** Don't proceed to Phase 2. Iterate Phase 1 (better content, pricing, positioning).

### 10.2 Phase 2 red flags

- Watch churn > 30% in first 3 months (no sticky value)
- Pulse open rate < 30% (no engagement)
- 0 audit klienti upgradujú na Watch (no path)
- Cannot deliver monthly Health Snapshots on time

**Action:** Pause Pro launch. Fix Watch quality. Talk to churned klientov.

### 10.3 Phase 3 red flags

- Pro churn > 15% in first 3 months
- < 5 Pro klientov get to month 3 (insufficient validation)
- Tomas burnout severe (>30h/týž + Telekom impossible)
- Cannot deliver Action Reports on schedule

**Action:** Pause Enterprise. Hire support. Improve automation. Consider Telekom transition.

### 10.4 Universal red flag - kompletne ZASTAVIŤ

- Tomas health suffering
- Family relationships strained
- Telekom job in jeopardy
- Negative cash flow > 3 mesiace v rade

**Action:** Pause Mentivue, recover, restart later. Health first.

## 13. Bottom line - prečo phased hybrid

### 13.1 What you DON'T do

- ❌ Skok rovno do subscription-first (príliš rýchle)
- ❌ Pozícia ako "report business" forever (príliš obmedzené)
- ❌ Build everything perfect before launch (perfection killer)
- ❌ Hire team day 1 (premature burn)
- ❌ Quit Telekom hneď (no validation yet)

### 13.2 What you DO

- ✅ Launch v Týždeň 6 s minimum viable produkty
- ✅ Validate dopyt cez one-off audits (Phase 1)
- ✅ Add lightweight subscription (Phase 2) keď máš signal
- ✅ Scale up subscription (Phase 3) ako rastie demand
- ✅ Enterprise tier (Phase 4) keď máš sustainable operations
- ✅ Hire postupne, nie predčasne
- ✅ Telekom transition iba po validation

### 13.3 The win condition

Po 12 mesiacoch:
- **Reálne klienti platia subscription**
- **MRR €20-40k a rastie**
- **Mentivue je etablovaná autorita v SK AI search**
- **Tomas má volbu:** quit Telekom alebo pokračovať lifestyle business
- **Y2 trajectory clear**

Ten najlepší scenár nie je hyperbolic exit. Je to **mať option**. Phased hybrid ti dáva tú option.
